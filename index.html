About

What are we?

We are a non-profit club located in San Diego, California. We are specificaly located in Mesa Verde Middle School. We love programming, and want to share our love with others. We try to convey our passion and help others understand that passion. We teach a variety of languages, in order to help others understand the freedom and power you have while programming. We not only teach students how to simply code, we teach our students problem-solving skills, memorization, creativity, and how to have fun with something seemingly monotonous.
